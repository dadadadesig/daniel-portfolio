Case Study: Smart Parking Search

A solution to the problem of parking search in traffic congestion and creating a smooth user experience from the search process to payment.

Our Journey

Discover how we tackled the challenges of parking search to create an efficient and user-friendly solution.

The Problem

Parking search processes take a lot of time, creating traffic congestion, pollution, and discomfort among drivers.

Goals

  • Help drivers find parking quickly.
  • Ensure a smooth user experience from search to payment.
  • Motivate private parking owners to share their spots for profit.

User Personas

Introduce the primary personas that represent key user groups of your app. Explain their goals, frustrations, and background.

Persona 1: "Busy Commuter"

  • Goals: Quick parking, minimal cost, easy payment.
  • Frustrations: Traffic jams, complicated apps, high parking prices.
  • Motivation: Get to work on time without stress.

Persona 2: "Occasional Driver"

  • Goals: Infrequent use, wants a simple sign-up flow.
  • Frustrations: Dislikes advanced settings or too many steps.
  • Motivation: Quick, no-hassle parking once in a while.
