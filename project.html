Key research findings

User Distribution

40% of users are in city centers, with 35% in grid areas and 25% in off-grid locations

  • 40% In City
  • 35% In grid
  • 25% off grid

Time Impact

5-15 Minutes is the breaking point at which drivers give up on finding street parking and switch to expensive paid parking lots, highlighting the need for efficient parking solutions.

App usage

75% Are reported of not using a consistent app for parking search

Feature Priority

Real-time availability (80%) and vacancy alerts (70%) are the most desired features
